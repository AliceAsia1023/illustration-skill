# Composition Patterns

Pick one pattern per image.

## Workflow

Use when the concept is a sequence or handoff. Show the girl moving, pulling, routing, or transferring something through 2 to 4 stages.

## System Partial

Use when the concept is about one operating area in a larger system. Show a machine corner, funnel, bridge, gate, pipe, or container instead of a whole architecture.

## Before And After

Use when the concept is about change in quality, clarity, or state. Keep the contrast obvious and simple.

## Risk Point

Use when the concept is about breakpoints, pitfalls, leakage, or overload. Put the girl near the fragile point as an observer or fixer.

## Concept Metaphor

Use when the concept is abstract. Convert it into one low-tech physical object such as a bridge, press, ladder, tray, tank, valve, basket, or sieve.

## Layered Method

Use when the concept has 2 to 4 layers or stacked logic. Avoid drawing a full framework diagram.

## Tiny Comic

Use when a micro-sequence explains the point better than a single object. Keep it to 2 to 4 beats and preserve whitespace.
