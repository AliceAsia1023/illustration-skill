# Twin-Tail Girl IP

## Character Definition

The recurring character is a hand-drawn girl with two ponytails. She is a quiet system operator inside the illustration, not a mascot and not a decorative bystander.

## Visual Traits

- Very simple doodle-style line-drawn girl
- Two ponytails are the main recognition feature
- Round or slightly oval head shape
- Dot eyes or tiny short-line eyes, with a very small mouth
- Hair should look like quick black pen strokes rather than neat illustration hair
- Limbs should be single-stroke or near-single-stroke whenever possible
- Slightly awkward childlike proportions are preferred over pretty proportions
- Simple outfit only: T-shirt and skirt, dress, or overalls
- No elaborate accessories unless the concept requires one practical prop

## Expression And Temperament

- calm
- focused
- restrained
- slightly awkward in a believable way
- a little cold humor is acceptable

## Typical Responsibilities

Make her perform the central action:

- sorting inputs
- carrying materials
- pulling a path
- patching a bridge
- turning a judgment lever
- filtering messy notes through a funnel
- watching a risk point
- labeling evidence
- pushing items from A to B
- opening, closing, blocking, or redirecting a system path

## Hard Constraints

- Do not turn her into a cute mascot
- Do not make a full character portrait
- Do not use giant sparkling eyes
- Do not use heavy blush, frills, or magical-girl styling
- Do not draw polished anime hair, eyelashes, or delicate facial features
- Do not make the girl look pretty, elegant, or fashion-illustrated
- Do not let clothing dominate the image
- Do not let the character replace the structural metaphor

## Reference Feel

Target the feel of a quick black-marker family doodle:

- blunt and direct
- informal
- slightly clumsy
- expressive through pose, not facial detail
- readable in one glance

The girl should feel like she was sketched in a few strokes inside a concept drawing, not designed as a standalone character.

## Test

If you remove the girl and the image still communicates almost everything, rewrite the prompt so she becomes the action carrier.
