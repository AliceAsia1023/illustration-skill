# QA Checklist

Check all items before delivery.

- Is the canvas clearly 16:9 and horizontal?
- Is the background pure white with no texture, shadow, or gradient?
- Is the linework primarily thin black hand-drawn strokes?
- Is there enough whitespace?
- Does the image express exactly one core idea?
- Is the twin-tail girl carrying the core action instead of decorating the frame?
- Are the Chinese notes short and sparse?
- Are red, orange, and blue used sparingly?
- Does the result avoid PPT, infographic, classroom slide, and poster energy?
- Does the result avoid children-book sweetness and mascot cuteness?
- If the girl is removed, does the image lose a meaningful part of the concept? If not, revise.
